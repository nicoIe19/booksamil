DADDY

Harper thought it was all over. She thought that Mommy was gone, and that they would be safe. And that was true. Mommy was gone--only Harper ends up finding herself with someone just as bad...maybe far worse. Harper and Rosabeth find themselves in a place they can't escape. A place full of horror, schedules, and a strong smell of bleach...they find themselves in Daddy's house. Once she realizes what this place is made of, she looks for a way out. But this escape is nothing like Mommy's. This place has no way out, and the only way to be set free of this terror-filled home...is to kill its creator. (copied from Wattpad)
