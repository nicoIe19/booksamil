TRIGGER HAPPY

Vanessa who was an undercover assassin lived as part time bartender who hid her job from her husband, Tangerine. Tangerine had been saved by Vanessa when she was ordered to kill him and his partner, Lemon. At the last minute, she regretted it and her brothers life almost paid at that debt by her handler. Vanessa and Tangerine fell in love after that first interaction. (Lemon was the third wheel) The two found each other on a Bullet Train by accident. Hopefully the two would get out alive.
